Our Services

A different kind of intelligence and close protection company. One rooted in the military, but grounded in real-world experience. We’re friendly, approachable, with specialised skills and above all - highly professional. Experienced in providing a 360-degree service, covering everything from bespoke risk assessments to discreet close protection, linguistics, team tracking and surveillance from a large pool of security vetted and trusted operators - here’s how we think we can help you.

Close Protection

Avalon Security provides unique individuals and teams to meet your specific protection or security needs, in the UK or abroad. Our team blends seamlessly into a civilian discreet and professional profile.

Security Risk Management

Avalon's background and experience in military and civilian security management ensures a balance of security risk for public UK events through to activities in war zones and other hostile or high threat environments.

Surveillance & Intelligence

The Avalon surveillance operators deliver surveillance packages that adapt to the changing nature of the target. From static surveillance and photography at range to mobile tracking, in both the rural and urban environments.

Medical Support

Avalon provide specialist medics and paramedics, experienced in operating in hostile or remote environments they come with comprehensive skills and equipment to maintain safety.

Communications and IT

Avalon deliver a bespoke tracking service as well as communications and IT solutions for hostile or remote environments. This includes back-end support for the duration of your operation.

Access Consultancy and Logistics

Avalon provide experienced problem solvers and linguists as well as mountaineers and rope access technicians, that can enable you to operate in difficult environments.
